Research Vision

I am an observational astrophysicist dedicated to unraveling the cosmic history of star formation[cite: 516]. My research integrates data from the UV to the far-infrared to describe the Star Formation Rate Density ($\rho_{SFR}$) across cosmic time[cite: 13, 517].

Currently, my work focuses on the **reionization era ($z \sim 11$)** using high-resolution data from the JWST PRIMER survey[cite: 527, 528].

Leading Programs

NCN SONATA BIS

Evolution of Star Formation in the Early Universe with JWST

Establishing a new research team to analyze the large-area JWST NIRCam+MIRI imaging[cite: 527, 528]. We explore the star-forming main sequence and gas mass fractions of galaxies up to $z \sim 11$[cite: 532, 752].

NCN SONATA

Cosmic History of the SFRD

Investigating the evolution of star formation using ALMA and SCUBA-2 data[cite: 26, 237]. This project applies novel stacking and de-blending techniques to account for dust-obscured stellar emission[cite: 97, 492].
